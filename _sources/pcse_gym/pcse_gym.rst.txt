.. _pcse_gym:

PCSE Gym
========

The ``pcse_gym/`` folder is the `Open AI Gymansium <https://gymnasium.farama.org/index.html>`_ Wrapper around 
our version of the Python Crop Simulation Environment :ref:`pcse`. 

Within this module we include prespecified farming policies, a wide variety of environments, different reward wrappers for training RL Agents, and environment configuration.
